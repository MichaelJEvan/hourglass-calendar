About
Hourglass ⏳ Calendar
Version 1.0
Your personal hourglass & calendar for tracking the events that matter most. Supports multiple calendars, recurring events, date ranges, alerts, and live countdowns — all ticking in real time.
Moon phases, supermoons, and solstices & equinoxes calculated using Jean Meeus astronomical algorithms from Astronomical Algorithms (1991). Moon phases accurate to within ~2 minutes of US Naval Observatory data; solstices & equinoxes accurate to within minutes of official astronomical data. Sunrise & sunset times calculated using the NOAA/Spencer solar position algorithm (Spencer, 1971) based on your device location. 🌙🌕🌸☀️🍂❄️
Meteor shower dates based on established peak windows for the 12 major annual showers. ☄️
Earth perihelion & aphelion dates from the JPL DE405 ephemeris. Table courtesy of Fred Espenak, astropixels.com. 🌍
Lunar eclipses calculated using the Meeus algorithm. Solar eclipses from the NASA/Espenak Five Millennium Catalog of Solar Eclipses — accurate to within 1–2 km of path edges. "Eclipse Predictions by Fred Espenak and Jean Meeus (NASA/GSFC)". ☀️🌑

Quick Tips
  • The Astro calendar auto-populates with ☀️ Sunrise and 🌙 Sunset times. Set your location in ⚙ Settings → Astro Calendar.
  • Click any calendar day to add or view events.
  • Hover over a day to preview its events.
  • Use Add Event to set a date, all day or time, date range, recurrences (🔸 icons), and alert option.
  • Enable Alert on an event to get an in-app audible notification — the clock will flash and the Alert Sound repeats every 15 seconds until you cancel.
  • Use the calendar tabs to switch between calendars.
  • Use ⚙ Manage in the tab bar to add, rename, or delete calendars.
  • Your data saves automatically — use Export Backup to save a copy to iCloud, Documents, or any file location.
